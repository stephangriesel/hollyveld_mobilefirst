Shaun Gouws - Producer

The nice guy… but mess with his budget or schedule and hell knows no fury like a producer scorned. Executive Producer and founder of Hollyveld Films started off in the production of television promos, but soon went on to produce commercials. Following his own interest he started producing music videos in his spare time and later longer format television series. Throughout the years he has serviced clients like Sony-Ericsson, Coca Cola, M-TV, Edcon to name a few.
He has been described as man that can singlehandedly rule a small country.

Arnold Snyman – Producer

After several awards for best film at Innibos and broadcast deals Arnold decided to join the British Army to experience true human emotion. He is now known as the “Celluloid Soldier” in our circles. After years we have finally convinced him to return to sunny South Africa and all we can say is watch out for the emotional journey this man can conjure up when he’s behind a camera. His absolute passion for the outdoors and filmmaking also makes him our go-to guy when it comes to location shoots all across South Africa.

Lauren’s internet presence on YouTube is growing daily, with close to 1 000 000 channel views and a fan base that follows her dancing and choreography videos avidly.
